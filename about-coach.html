Coach Jrob works with beginners, youth athletes, adults, and aspiring fighters who want to build real boxing skill through structure, repetition, and direct feedback. His coaching focuses on fundamentals first — stance, balance, defense, movement, control, and confidence before power.

Coaching Philosophy

Structure First. Confidence Built Through Work.

Coach Jrob’s coaching philosophy is rooted in discipline, fundamentals, and personal development. Every session is built to help students understand proper technique, improve their conditioning, develop confidence, and learn how to stay composed under pressure.

Discipline

Training that teaches consistency, focus, accountability, and self-control.

Confidence

Coaching that helps students believe in their ability to learn, improve, and push through challenges.

Fundamentals

Boxing instruction built on proper stance, movement, defense, technique, conditioning, and control.

Growth

A training environment focused on becoming stronger physically, mentally, and personally.

BDD Boxing is not built around ego or intimidation. It is built around structure, effort, accountability, mentorship, and real growth.

The Mission Behind The Coaching

More Than Training. A Standard For Growth.

“BDD Boxing exists to empower individuals through discipline, fitness, and mentorship by using the sport of boxing to build confidence, character, and stronger communities.”

Coach Jrob’s role is to carry that mission into every session. Whether working with youth, adults, beginners, or aspiring fighters, the goal is not only to teach boxing, but to help each person build structure, resilience, self-respect, and purpose.
