    Frequently Asked Questions

    Everything you need to know to get started with your training path.

    Do I need boxing experience?

    No, absolutely not. Over 70% of our clients start with zero prior boxing experience. Our coaching is entirely individualized; we break down stance, guard, and punch mechanics step-by-step at your own comfortable learning speed.

    What should I bring?

    Wear comfortable athletic clothes, clean gym shoes, and bring a water bottle. We provide clean boxing gloves and hand wraps for your initial sessions. Once you decide to train consistently, we'll help you select your own personal wraps and gloves.

    Is this for fitness or real boxing?

    It is both. We teach actual, combat-proven boxing fundamentals (how to pivot, slip, roll, hook, block, and coordinate footwork) but scale the workouts to deliver an exceptional aerobic and anaerobic conditioning result. You learn real skill while getting in peak athletic shape.

    Do you train kids?

    Yes, our Youth Boxing path is specifically tailored for children and teenagers (ages 8-16). These sessions emphasize safety, coordination, focus, physical conditioning, self-defense confidence, and developing outstanding self-discipline.

    Can I train if I’m out of shape?

    Yes. Boxing conditioning is highly scalable. We modify the intensity of mitt drills, heavy bag work, and cardiovascular movements to match your current fitness level, helping you build stamina, strength, and endurance safely and quickly.

    How do I book?

    It's simple. Fill out the booking form below, or click any of the "Book Session" buttons across the site. We will reach out within 24 hours to schedule a time that fits your calendar and assign you to your training path.
