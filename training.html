Your First Session

Simple, Structured, And Beginner-Friendly.

Your first session is built to help Coach Jrob understand your goals, experience level, fitness background, and comfort with boxing. No pressure. No ego. Just a structured introduction to the fundamentals.

01

Talk Through Your Goals

Start with a quick conversation about your experience, fitness level, goals, and what you want to build through boxing.

02

Learn Your Stance

Build your boxing base with stance, balance, guard position, and foot placement.

03

Build Fundamentals

Learn basic punches, movement, defense, breathing, and control through clear instruction.

04

Work Mitts Or Bag

Apply the fundamentals through mitt work, bag work, or simple drills based on your level.

05

Condition With Purpose

Use boxing-specific conditioning to build endurance, strength, coordination, and mental toughness.

06

Leave With A Plan

Finish with feedback, corrections, and a clear next step for your training path.

Training Standard

Every Session Has A Purpose.

BDD Boxing training is built on consistent effort, structured progression, and real boxing skill development.

Fundamentals First

Stance, balance, movement, defense, control, and clean technique come before power.

Discipline Through Repetition

Progress comes from showing up, learning the details, and building habits through consistent work.

Confidence Through Skill

Students gain confidence by learning how to move, defend, think, and respond under pressure.

Growth Beyond The Gym

The structure built through boxing carries into school, work, family, and daily life.
