Training Options

Training Options

Each program is built to meet people where they are and help them grow with structure, accountability, and purpose.

Private Boxing Training

Who It Is For For beginners, adults, youth athletes, and individuals who want focused one-on-one coaching tailored to their goals.
What It Builds Technique, confidence, conditioning, discipline, footwork, defense, and personal accountability.

Private Boxing Training gives each person focused attention from Coach Jrob so they can improve at their own pace. Sessions are built around skill development, fitness, confidence, and structure while helping each person become stronger inside and outside the gym.

Start Private Training

Beginner Boxing

Who It Is For For people stepping into boxing for the first time or anyone who wants to learn proper fundamentals in a structured environment.
What It Builds Stance, balance, basic punches, movement, defense, confidence, coordination, and discipline.

Beginner Boxing helps new students learn the sport the right way. The focus is on building a strong foundation, developing confidence, understanding proper technique, and creating a disciplined training rhythm without intimidation.

Start Beginner Boxing

Youth Boxing

Who It Is For For kids and teens who need structure, confidence, discipline, fitness, mentorship, and a positive training environment.
What It Builds Respect, focus, self-control, confidence, coordination, discipline, fitness, and character.

Youth Boxing uses the sport of boxing as a tool for growth. Students learn proper boxing fundamentals while building discipline, respect, confidence, and accountability through structured coaching and positive mentorship.

Ask About Youth Boxing

Boxing Fitness

Who It Is For For adults and beginners who want to get in shape, relieve stress, build strength, and improve confidence through boxing-based training.
What It Builds Conditioning, strength, endurance, coordination, stress relief, confidence, and mental toughness.

Boxing Fitness combines boxing movements, conditioning, and structured training to help people build a stronger body and sharper mindset. This program is ideal for anyone who wants the benefits of boxing without needing to compete.

Start Boxing Fitness

Fighter Development

Who It Is For For aspiring fighters, experienced athletes, and students who want to sharpen their boxing skills for sparring or competition.
What It Builds Advanced technique, sparring readiness, conditioning, defense, footwork, fight IQ, discipline, and competitive confidence.

Fighter Development is designed for athletes who want to take boxing seriously. Training focuses on technical growth, conditioning, defensive awareness, sparring preparation, discipline, and the mental sharpness needed to perform under pressure.

Train Like A Fighter

Small Group Training

Who It Is For For friends, families, youth groups, adults, and small teams who want structured boxing training in a focused group setting.
What It Builds Teamwork, confidence, accountability, conditioning, boxing fundamentals, discipline, and motivation.

Small Group Training gives people the energy of training together while still keeping the structure and attention of coached boxing instruction. It is built for groups that want to grow stronger, sharper, and more disciplined together.

Book Small Group Training
